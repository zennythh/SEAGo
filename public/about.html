About SEAGo

SEAGo is a student-built platform created to showcase the beautiful and culturally rich countries of Southeast Asia. Whether you’re dreaming of visiting bustling markets in Vietnam, island hopping in the Philippines, or exploring ancient temples in Thailand, SEAGo offers inspiration and information at your fingertips.

Our Mission

To promote awareness, appreciation, and tourism of Southeast Asia by providing interactive maps, cultural insights, and travel tips—all in one accessible site.
